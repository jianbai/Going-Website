How Going works

Go

Press "Go" and Going will match you with 3 people around you based on your age and gender preferences

Go out

Matches disappear when the weekend ends. Chat with your matches in the app and make plans to go somewhere cool!

Go again

Choose who you want to keep in touch with by adding them to your friends list. Go again next weekend to make new matches and grow your friends list!
