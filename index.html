Go

Press "Go" and Going will match you with 3 people around you based on your age and gender preferences.

Go out

Chat with your matches in the app and make plans to go somewhere cool! Matches disappear when the weekend ends.

Go again

Choose who you want to keep in touch with by adding them to your friends list. Go again next weekend to meet more people!

Frequently or rarely asked questions

Is this a dating app?

Nope!

So then who is this app for?

Going is for people who want to meet people. If (a) you like meeting new people and (b) you're free this weekend, then Going is for you!

How do matches work?

Matches are groups of 4 people that are free to hang out this weekend. Matches are made based on age and gender preferences.

Why do matches only last for the weekend?

Matches disappear every week so you can make new matches and meet more people.

How do I add friends?

When your matches disappear, you'll be prompted to choose who you want to keep as friends.

Why groups of 4?

Because 4 is a magical number.

Why can't I choose my matches?

Going is a place where unlikely friendships are made. You set your preferences based on age and gender, and chance takes care of the rest!

Who are you people? Why are you doing this?

We are concerned citizens who miss the times when you didn't need an excuse to hang out with strangers and make new friends. So we imagined this big orange button that summoned new people and made it into this app.
